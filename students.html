    • Eskisehir Technical University Facilities

    Dining Services

    In order to ensure that our students have access to healthy and high-quality meals, food services are provided at our university's Two Eylul Campus and Porsuk Campus. In addition, social facilities such as canteens, cafeterias, and dining halls also serve our students.

    Library Facilities

    With a strong collection of electronic resources, the central library and reading room offer services to students and academics with access to over 130,000 e-books and more than 30,000 printed books. Remote access to the library’s electronic resources is also available.

    Transportation

    Our campuses are accessible via buses and minibuses departing from various points of the city. Students meet their accommodation needs through KYK dormitories, private dormitories, and apartment residences located in different parts of the city. There is also a KYK dormitory located near the campus.

    Health Services

    Both our students and staff can benefit from the University’s Family Health Center. Currently, two outpatient clinics are actively operating, providing services such as medical examinations, prescription issuance, injections, wound care, and blood tests.

    Psychological Counseling and Guidance Services

    The Psychological Counseling and Guidance Unit offers services to support individuals in their social, emotional, and career development areas according to their needs and preferences. The unit also organizes seminars, workshops, and informational meetings.

    Student Clubs

    Our university’s student clubs are here to help students develop individual talents, gain teamwork skills, and meet their social and cultural needs. The university supports students both in joining existing clubs and in establishing their own.
