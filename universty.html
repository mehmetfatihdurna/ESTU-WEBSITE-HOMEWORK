    • Established by Law No. 7141 published in the Resmî Gazete dated May 18, 2018 and numbered 30425, Eskişehir Technical University is located in the heart of Eskişehir — a city of science, culture, and youth — and operates across three campuses. The university comprises 5 faculties, 3 vocational schools, 1 school, 3 graduate institutes, and 8 research and application centers. Eskişehir Technical University provides education and conducts research through its well-equipped academic units. With a competent academic staff who closely follow developments in their respective fields and dedicate their time to students, the university aims to prepare students as well-equipped and competitive individuals for the future in creative and dynamic learning environments. The operation of Hasan Polatkan International Airport, located in our 2 Eylül Campus, is managed by our Faculty of Aeronautics and Astronautics. Various international flights are conducted to and from Hasan Polatkan Airport by different airline companies on certain days of the week.
